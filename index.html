Our Destinations

For more than 25 years, the company behind the award-winning Chiva-Som has been a pioneer in transformative wellness practices to global acclaim. Its commitment to an innovative, holistic approach which expertly balances mind, body and spirit, continues to this day with its flagship resort in Hua Hin, Thailand and the latest addition to the Chiva-Som family - Zulal Wellness Resort in Khasooma, Qatar.

Thailand

Chiva-Som Hua Hin

Chiva-Som in Hua Hin, Thailand, is Asia’s renowned and pioneering destination wellness resort. The beachfront resort’s programme brings together Western diagnostic skills and Eastern philosophies to revitalise the mind, body and spirit.

Qatar

Zulal Wellness Resort

Zulal Wellness Resort is a pioneering destination in the Middle East that blends Traditional Arabic & Islamic Medicine with a holistic health and wellbeing philosophy, established by Chiva-Som, the internationally-acclaimed wellness resort in Thailand.

Sustainability at Chiva-Som

We believe personal wellness goes hand-in-hand with environmental wellness and that companies should conduct themselves using responsible and ethical business practices, policies and initiatives. That’s why, since opening in 1995, we have nurtured a healthy, safe and vibrant community, operated as an integral part of Hua Hin and played our part in protecting the Earth.
